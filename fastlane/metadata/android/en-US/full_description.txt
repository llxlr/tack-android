<p><b>🎵 A Metronome You’ll Actually Love Using</b></p>
<p>Tack is more than just a metronome — it’s a sleek, highly customizable rhythm companion built for musicians who care about precision <i>and</i> aesthetics. Whether you’re practicing alone or performing live, Tack helps you stay in perfect time without distractions.</p>
<p><b>📱 On Your Phone — Powerful, Elegant, Thoughtful</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Beautiful beat visualization with changeable emphasizes and subdivisions</li>
<li>Song library for saving and organizing metronome configurations</li>
<li>Options for count-in, duration, incremental tempo change, muted beats and swing</li>
<li>Settings for flash screen, volume, audio latency correction and elapsed time</li>
<li>Support for dynamic color, dynamic contrast and large screens</li>
<li>100% ad-free — no analytics, no interruptions</li>
</ul>
<p><b>⌚️ On Your Wrist — Best-in-Class for Wear OS</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Quick tempo changes with intuitive picker and a separate tap screen</li>
<li>Advanced beat customization with changeable emphasizes and subdivisions</li>
<li>Bookmarks for tempo, beats and subdivisions</li>
<li>Settings for flash screen, volume and audio latency correction</li>
</ul>
